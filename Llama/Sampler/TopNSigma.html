    Overview

    Top-N Sigma sampler

    The Top-N Sigma sampler selects tokens based on their distance from the mean in terms of standard deviations. This is based on the paper "Top-nÏƒ: Not All Logits Are You Need" (https://arxiv.org/pdf/2411.07641).

    Example:

    sampler = Llama::Sampler::TopNSigma.new(2.0) # Keep tokens within 2 standard deviations
